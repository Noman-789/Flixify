04 — FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS.

Everything you need to know about our referral program. Can't find an answer? Reach out to us directly.

WHO CAN JOIN THE REFERRAL PROGRAM?

Anyone can join — past clients, industry peers, Shopify partners, or anyone with a network of ecommerce business owners. There's no purchase required to participate.

WHEN DO I GET PAID?

Rewards are processed within 48 hours of the referred brand signing a project contract. Gold and Platinum partners get even faster payouts — 24 hours and same-day respectively.

WHAT COUNTS AS A SUCCESSFUL REFERRAL?

A referral is considered successful when the referred brand signs a paid project contract with Flixifys. Just signing up or having a consultation call doesn't qualify — the project must begin.

IS THERE A LIMIT ON HOW MUCH I CAN EARN?

No limits at all. Refer 5 brands or 50 — every successful referral earns you a reward. Plus, as you refer more, you automatically unlock higher reward tiers.

HOW DO I TRACK MY REFERRALS?

Every referrer gets access to a personal dashboard where you can track link clicks, sign-ups, project status, and earnings in real time. Gold and Platinum members also receive monthly email reports.

HOW ARE REWARDS PAID OUT?

Rewards are paid via direct bank transfer (India) or PayPal/Wise (international). You'll need to submit your payment details once before your first payout. All subsequent payouts are automatic.
