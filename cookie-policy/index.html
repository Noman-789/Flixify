Cookie Policy

Learn how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website to improve your experience.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your overall experience.

Cookies help us remember who you are, what you prefer, and what you've done before — so every visit is smoother and more relevant to you.

Types of Cookies We Use

TypePurposeDuration
Strictly Necessary Essential for the website to function properly Session
Performance Track traffic and usage analytics 1–2 Years
Functionality Remember preferences and settings 1 Year
Targeting / Advertising Deliver relevant advertisements 1–2 Years
Third Party Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc. Varies

How to Manage Cookies

You can block, delete, or manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking certain cookies may affect some website functionality.

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Mozilla Firefox

Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Apple Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Cookies → Manage
