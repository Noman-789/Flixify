Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Shopify services, process, and support.

Getting Started
01
What services does Flixifys offer?
Flixifys is a Shopify-focused digital studio offering end-to-end ecommerce services including store design, custom development, speed optimization, mobile optimization, platform migration, and ongoing support. We specialize in building high-converting Shopify stores tailored to your brand and business goals.
02
How do I get started with a project?
Simply fill out our contact form or reach out via WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free consultation call to understand your requirements, after which you'll receive a detailed project proposal with scope, timeline, and pricing.
03
Do you offer a free consultation?
Yes! We offer a complimentary 15-30 minute consultation call where we discuss your business goals, current challenges, and how we can help. There's no obligation — it's simply an opportunity for us to understand your needs and for you to evaluate if we're the right fit.
Pricing & Payments
04
How much does a Shopify store cost?
Pricing depends entirely on the project scope — a basic store setup starts at a different point than a fully custom-built store with advanced features. We provide transparent, itemized proposals after understanding your requirements. There are no hidden fees. Contact us for a personalized quote.
05
What is your payment structure?
Typically, we work with a 50% advance to begin the project and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can structure milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfers, UPI, and other digital payment methods. All payments are invoiced properly.
06
Do you offer refunds?
Since we create customized digital work specific to each client, refunds are generally not available once work has started. However, if you have concerns at any stage, we encourage you to discuss them with us — we're committed to finding solutions. Refund requests are evaluated case-by-case within 7 days of payment.
Process & Timeline
07
How long does it take to build a Shopify store?
Timelines vary based on complexity. A standard store with theme customization typically takes 1-3 weeks. A fully custom-designed store with advanced features may take 4-8 weeks. We provide specific timelines in our project proposal and keep you updated throughout the process.
08
How many revisions are included?
Each project stage (design, development) includes a defined number of revision rounds as specified in the proposal — typically 2-3 rounds per stage. Additional revisions beyond the scope can be accommodated at an agreed-upon hourly rate. We focus on getting things right through clear communication early on.
09
Will I be able to manage the store myself after launch?
Absolutely. We build stores using Shopify's native interface wherever possible, so you can easily manage products, orders, and content. We also provide a handover session and documentation to help you get comfortable with your store's backend.
Technical & Support
10
Do you provide post-launch support?
Yes! We offer ongoing support packages that include bug fixes, minor updates, performance monitoring, and technical assistance. Details of post-launch support are outlined in each project proposal. We also offer ad-hoc support for clients not on a monthly plan.
11
Can you migrate my existing store to Shopify?
Yes, we handle migrations from WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, Wix, and custom-built platforms. The migration includes products, customers, orders, content, and SEO data. We ensure minimal downtime and thorough testing before going live on the new platform.
12
Which third-party apps do you work with?
We have experience integrating with a wide range of Shopify apps including Razorpay, Stripe, Shiprocket, Klaviyo, Gorgias, Loox, Judge.me, Recharge, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and many more. If you need a specific app integrated, let us know — we'll make it work.
General
13
Do you work with international clients?
Yes, we work with clients globally. Our communication is primarily through WhatsApp, email, and video calls, which makes geography irrelevant. We're flexible with time zones and ensure smooth collaboration regardless of location.
14
Can I see your previous work or case studies?
Yes! We share relevant case studies and portfolio examples during our consultation call, tailored to your industry and project type. Some projects are confidential, but we always have representative examples to share. Visit our blog for insights and project highlights.
